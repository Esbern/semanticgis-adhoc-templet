# Data Genesis: cultural_amenities.geojson

- **Provenance**: Queried from OpenStreetMap via OSMnx Realisation of Services and Amenities.
- **Creation History**: Extracted by AI script `jobs/cultural_amenities/fetch_amenities.py` using `data/edits/raffen.gpkg` as the spatial boundary.
- **Layers**: Single GeoJSON FeatureCollection containing cultural amenities (amenity=arts_centre, theatre, etc. and tourism=museum, gallery, artwork).
- **Edits made**: None. Raw dataset subset geographically to the human-refined Refshaleøen boundary.
